Introduction

The NENU-ICB is a research group focusing on human complex diseases research and relevant drug target discovering starting from Protein Interactome Networks. Many research fields are involved in our works, including biological big data, computational methods and algorithms. We are trying our best to offer computational tools and biological data services for the better lives tomorrow!
